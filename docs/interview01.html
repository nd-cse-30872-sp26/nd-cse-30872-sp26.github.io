Next week, you will have the opportunity to demonstrate your mastery of the skills and concepts discussed in Unit 01: Data Structures and Algorithms to the instructor or a teaching assistant in a 30 minute in-person coding interview.

This interview involve:

  1. Code Review: You will be asked to discuss the data structure and algorithm you used in a previous Challenge.

  2. Live Challenge: You will be asked to solve a programming Challenge.

Scheduling¶

To schedule a coding interview appointment, put your netid (ie. pbui) in an open cell in a timeslot with an interviewer in the following:

Interview 01 Spreadsheet

The location of the interviewer can be found in the Locations tab of the Interview 01 Spreadsheet.

Scheduling Deadline¶

For planning purposes, please schedule your Interview 01 appointment by midnight, Friday, February 27.

You may only signup for one appointment timeslot.

Consulting Resources¶

As with your Reading and Homework assignments, you are allowed to consult the course texts, slides, notes, the Internet, and even AI tools. However, you cannot communicate with anyone else nor can you ask an AI tool to generate a solution.

Be mindful that the interviewer is assessing your proficiency and how much you have internalized. Over reliance on any external resource will be viewed negatively in regards to your mastery of the material.
